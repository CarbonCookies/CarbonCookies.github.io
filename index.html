Welcome

to a complete guide on everything you can do on the
Night-Stalkers Freebuild server.
Guides, Tips, what to do etc. for beginners and first timers.

Don't Do(s)

You will be kicked, banned, or toggled off in building by doing the following:

1. Griefing
Destroying & messing around with someone else's buildings without their permission isn't allowed.
2. Spamming Blocks
Putting a lot of random blocks in the map isn't allowed.

See a longer list in the "Rules" tab.

Where to build

There are several flat areas where you can build. Flat maps are Free to build areas for anyone. You could teleport into the area by using /gt area ex: /gt a1 If there no more spaces to build, You could always ask for help in game or chat in the shoutbox of N-S Forums.

See a longer list of commands in the "Commands" tab.

Protected areas

There areas are places in the map where only Builders and up can build and destroy blocks on. If you can't build in some area, then you are probably in a protected area or you are toggled off in building.

Builders

Builders are people who can use special commands for building. Apply for builder here
There are higher positions oter than Builders, these are Guards & Admins. They can help ecen if they are not in game, so feel free to ask for help inside the game.

Show-off your builds

Have you ever built an awesome structure inside the N-S map? Then this is your chance to be acknowledged. Showing off your buildings increases you chances of becoming a builder. Show off your builds here.
